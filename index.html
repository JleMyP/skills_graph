A visualisation of developer skills in demand

We asked ourselves: what are the kinds of professionals that software companies are looking for? What are the skills in demand? To answer it, Andrei scraped tens of thousands of job postings, connecting pairs of skills into clusters. Below is an interactive synthesis of these findings, updated monthly.

  • The bigger the node, the more in demand the skill.
  • The wider the link, the closer the nodes, the more related are the two skills (seen together).
