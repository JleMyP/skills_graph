A visualisation of skills in demand

We asked ourselves: What skills every good Python programmer should have? What skills are in demand? To answer it, we scraped thousands of job postings, connecting skills into one big skills graph. So if you want to evolve as a professional, you can use this as a hint.

  • The bigger the node, the more in demand the skill.
  • The wider the link, the closer the nodes, the more related are the two skills (seen together).
